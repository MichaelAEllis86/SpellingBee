This is my project for Springboard Capstone 1, and I'm very proud to share it with everyone! This is a game that is based on the New York Times wordbuilder game the Spellingbee! For those unfamilar with the game the rules are posted below, alongside a glossary of game terms. Scroll down for rules, glossary of terms and ranks, and finally for the hints!

Game Rules

Create words from the pool of letters in the hive!

  • Each word must include the center letter
  • Letters can be used more than once
  • Score points by making words!
  • Each word's point value is equal to its length
  • Score more points to increase your rank for a given game
  • Ranks are based on your percentage of points scored out of the total points available for a given hive!
  • Hives/Gameboards vary because they are randomly generated! Some have many words that can be created, others might only have a few!
  • If you're feeling stuck you can always check out the hints page for your current Hive/board! Hints...but since you are on the hints page you will just end up back here 🐝
  • If you get stuck or get sick of your current letters, you can always start a new game from your user hub! You can revisit your games at anytime!

    • Glossary of Terms

    Hive/Board-The Hive is your game letter pool

    Center_letter/Center Hive-Refers to the letter in the center of the hive and is indicated in yellow. It must be used in the construction of any word

    Pangram-A special type of word that incorporates every letter in the hive at least once! We track your pangrams in the game stats. It should be noted that not all boards necessarily have pangrams by default

    Perfect Pangram-A special type of pangram where each letter is used exactly once! Worth 7 extra points or double points (functionality and tracking under construction!)

    BingoA special Hive/Board !All seven letters in the puzzle are used to start at least one word in the word list (functionality and tracking under construction!)
